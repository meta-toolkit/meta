template<class DerivedIndex>
template<class Derived, uint32_t MaxSize = 1000>
class meta::index::disk_index< DerivedIndex >::chunk_handler< Derived, MaxSize >

A handler for writing chunks to disk during the tokenization process.

This is a CRTP base class, whose derived classes must implement the handle_doc() and chunk() methods, as well as a proper destructor.
